Protection Duas

  • Dua for Protection from Evil Eye
    اللّهُـمّ إِنّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ عَيْنٍ لَمَّا تَرَى
    "Allahumma inni a'udhu bika min 'aynin laa tarah."
    "O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the evil eye that cannot be seen."
    Reference: Sahih Muslim, Book 26, Hadith 5404
  • Dua for Protection from Harm
    بِسْمِ اللّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَ لَا فِي السَّمَاء
    "Bismillahi alladhi laa yadurru ma'asmihi shay'un fi al-ardhi wa laa fi as-samaa."
    "In the name of Allah, with Whose name nothing is harmful on earth or in the sky."
    Reference: Sahih Muslim, Hadith 2716
  • Dua for Protection Before Sleeping
    اللّهُـمّ قِنِي عَذَابَكَ يَوْمَ تَجْمَعُ عِبَادَكَ
    "Allahumma qini 'adhabaka yawm tajma'u 'ibadaka."
    "O Allah, protect me from Your punishment on the day You gather Your servants."
    Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari, Hadith 6310
  • Dua for Protection from Anxiety
    اللّهُـمّ رَبِّ أَذْهِبْ عَنِّي هَمِّي وَغَمِّي
    "Allahumma rabbi adhhib 'anni hammi wa ghammi."
    "O Allah, Lord, remove from me my anxiety and grief."
    Reference: Sahih al-Bukhari, Hadith 6369
